Improving access to regulations and guidance for farmers

Under the government's Future Farming Programme, Defra are overhauling the way that farmers receive payments and grants.

We Are Margot led a discovery to help Defra understand how farmers currently access regulations and rules that govern rural payments.

We ran research sessions with diverse range of farmers across England, including those who are hard to reach and who have low digital literacy.
